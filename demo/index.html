SOCIAL MINUS SPYING

social sharing buttons that respect your damn privacy

QUICK START


1) Download S-S.js, and embed it anywhere on your web page.
<script src="S-S.js"></script>

2) Insert the following snippet where you want your social buttons:
<div class="s-s" data-type="facebook"></div>
<div class="s-s" data-type="twitter"></div>

3) That's it! You'll get privacy-respecting share buttons like these:
Not only are they more customizeable and more lightweight than the official social sharing widgets, your audience won't get dozens of third-party spies tailing them, just because they viewed a website with share buttons.

No longer do you have to opt-out of getting stalked. With S-S, it's opt-in!

THE WHY


I love sharing information! But I should have control over what I share.

That control over your generosity is the difference between letting a friend crash at your place, and them breaking into your apartment... then raiding your fridge for cookies, leaving a personalized ad for Oreos, and exiting through a backdoor that they secretly installed in everyone's houses.

THE HOW


No iframes or cookies. Proxy server. Static URL. AJAX.
This is to show how simple it is to help reclaim a web that respects privacy and autonomy.
