SOCIAL MINUS SPYING

social sharing buttons that respect your damn privacy

I love sharing information! But I should have control over what I share.

That control over your generosity is the difference between letting a friend crash at your place, and them breaking into your apartment... then raiding your cookie jar, leaving a personalized ad for Oreos, and exiting through a backdoor that they secretly installed in everyone's houses.

Social Minus Spying gives the functionality of a Facebook/Twitter/Whatever button, without your browser directly touching their servers! Whaaaaaaaa

Some sites don't respect that. A Facebook "Like" button tracks you even if you're not logged in. Social Minus Spying is an open-source, privacy-friendly replacement for the standard social sharing buttons. S-S does this, and that. Let your fans share your work, without letting them get tracked unknowingly.
